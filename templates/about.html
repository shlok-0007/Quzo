About Quzo

Quzo is a modern, interactive platform designed to streamline the process of creating, managing, and taking quizzes for educational and professional environments.

Our mission is to provide an intuitive and powerful tool for both professors and students. Professors can dynamically create quizzes, view detailed analytics on student performance, and even edit quizzes in real-time.
